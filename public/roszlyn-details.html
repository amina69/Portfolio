About The Project

The project is focused on developing a fully functional medical system to facilitate seamless interactions between patients and doctors.

My primary responsibility is designing the mobile interface for patient users, enabling features such as teleconsultataion, real-time vitals monitoring, AI-driven insights, and personalized action plans

Problem Statement
Healthcare systems often face challenges in ensuring efficient and seamless communication between patients and doctors. There is a need for a centralized platform that provides real-time health data, actionable insights, and tailored healthcare plans to empower patients and improve medical outcomes.
There is a need for an intuitive and user-friendly application that caters to individuals of all age groups and diverse cultural backgrounds.
Research Methods
User-centric design approach to understand patient needs and preferences
Utilization of AI algorithms for generating personalized insights and recommendations.
Prototyping and iterative testing of the mobile interface to ensure usability and accessibility.
Findings
Patients value real-time feedback on their health status, which fosters proactive health management.
AI-driven insights significantly enhance user engagement by providing actionable and personalized recommendations.
Seamless interaction between patients and healthcare providers improves medical care efficiency and satisfaction.
